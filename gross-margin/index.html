Gross Margin

Overview

Here you can see the details about Profit & Loss’ gross margin for each month of the current year.

A project’s gross margin is the difference between its revenues and its external costs.

Three important indicators are given in the first rows:

  • Total: the sum of all your projects’ gross margin, per month

  • Bluesky: the dfference between revenues’ bluesky and external costs’ bluesky

  • Delta revenues target: the dfference between revenues’ delta revenues target and external costs’ delta revenues target
